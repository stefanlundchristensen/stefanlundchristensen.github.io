# Hello You All

TL;DR: Hi, I’m Stefan, a quantum physicist turned payments geek. I’ve had the privilege of working as a consultant at McKinsey and being part of the team that built Pleo, a ompany revolutionizing business spending. Outside of work, I’m a father, a lover of gin and coffee, a budding natural wine enthusiast, and an aspiring road cyclist. In this blog, I’ll be sharing my experiences and insights on a range of topics. I invite you to join me on this exciting journey.

Hello there! I’m Stefan, and I’m thrilled to have you here. If you’ve ever wondered what quantum physics, music, payment systems, and personal passions have in common, you’re in the right place.

Who Am I?

I’m a man of many hats - a builder, a geek, a quantum physicist, a music enthusiast, a father, and a husband. My insatiable curiosity has always driven me to understand how things work - from the tiniest subatomic particles to the most complex systems.

My Journey Through Quantum Physics

My passion for science led me to pursue a degree in quantum physics. I started my career as a researcher, exploring the mysteries of the universe and pushing the boundaries of what we know.

Diving into the World of Payments

After my stint in research, I transitioned into a consultant role at McKinsey, where I focused on payment systems. I was fascinated by the complexity and the constant evolution of this industry. I worked on projects that tackled challenges in the payments landscape and helped businesses navigate through them.

Building Pleo From the Ground Up

My journey in the payments industry led me to Pleo, a company that’s revolutionizing business spending. At Pleo, I’ve had the privilege of leading a team of talented individuals. Together, we’ve built innovative solutions that make business spending smarter, faster, and simpler. It’s been an exciting journey, filled with challenges and triumphs, and I’m proud of what we’ve achieved.

A Glimpse into My Personal Life

Beyond my professional pursuits, I’m a proud father to Bille, a gin and coffee enthusiast, a budding natural wine aficionado, and an aspiring road cyclist. Being a father has been my greatest joy and challenge. It’s taught me patience, empathy, and the art of juggling multiple tasks at once.

As for my love for gin and coffee, it’s more than just about the taste. It’s about the process - the art and science that goes into making each cup or glass. Recently, I’ve also started exploring the world of natural wine. It’s been an exciting journey, filled with unique flavors and fascinating stories.

And when I’m not savoring a cup of coffee or a glass of wine, you’ll find me on my bike. I’ve always had a desire to be an active road cyclist. It’s not just about the physical challenge, but also the sense of freedom and adventure that comes with each ride.

What’s In Store For You?

Through this blog, I’ll be sharing my experiences, insights, and thoughts on a range of topics, including science, technology, payments, and the world around us. I’m always learning and exploring new ideas, and I can’t wait to share them with you.

Some of the topics I’ll be covering in my blog.

Let’s Embark on This Journey Together

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me a little better. I hope you’ll join me on this exciting journey. Feel free to leave comments, share your thoughts, or even suggest topics you’d like me to cover. I’d love to hear from you!
